About Hridi's Henna

Welcome to the innovative world of henna art & design, combining fusion designs from Arabic, Indian to Moghul.I'm Farjana Yeasmin Hridi, owner of Hridi's Henna. I have been running Hridi's Henna for the past 5 years, although I have been practicing mehendi for over 10 years! I absolutely love working with all of my brides, hand designing and packages gigts to be sent around the world, and being able to teach others who have an interest. I an humble with all the opportunities that i have so far, that I have been able to create a business from my talent and get to take it acress the world. Hridi's Henna offers a highly personalised and bespoke service to suit your individual needs and budgets. Hridi's Henna is a new generation of artists, using unique techniques together with natural ingredients for the mehndi, to add depth to its colour for your special occasions to last for days to come.
